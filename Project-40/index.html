Pasta Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 200g Fettuccine Pasta
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 2 cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • ¾ cup Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • Fresh Parsley (garnish)

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and sauté garlic for 1 minute.
  3. Pour in cream and simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add Parmesan cheese and stir until melted and smooth.
  5. Add pasta to the sauce and mix well. Season with salt & pepper.
  6. Plate and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve hot.
